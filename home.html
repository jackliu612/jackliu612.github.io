I've found a love for the theoretical side of Computer Science analyzing algorithms, but also enjoy applying my practical skills on (sometimes wacky) side projects

I also currently teach programming classes in Python and Java for middle school students.

Outside of the classroom, I am the president of theCourseForum which is a course review platform used by many in the University of Virginia community. This involved managing DevOps, site maintenance, and semesterly data updates while also becoming familiar with Docker, Google Cloud, and Digital Ocean.
